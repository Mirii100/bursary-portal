How to Apply for Bursary

Follow these simple steps to submit your application through the Alexia's Global Tech Portal.

1
Account Setup

Register an account and complete your 4-step student profile including residency and guardian details.

2
Document Upload

Prepare clear copies of your Student ID, Fee Structure, and Admission Letter for the current academic year.

3
Apply Online

Navigate to 'My Portal', select the academic year, enter the requested amount and upload your documents.

4
Track Status

Monitor your dashboard for real-time progress. You will receive SMS/Email alerts once vetting is complete.
